Days of year, month and day of month.

Uses the standard library's "make time" function to make the number of days for a year, month and month day. Divides the seconds by 86,400 (the number of seconds in a day) in order to answer days rather than seconds.

Parameters
yearYear from 1970.
monOne-based month. Converts to a zero-based month.
mdayDay of the month starting at one.
Note
The standard library's mktime() function has a prescribed minimum start time of 1970. Times cannot precede the first day of that year.
